Firecracker Chicken Wrap

These Firecracker Chicken Wraps are everything I crave when I want something spicy, crispy, and comforting but still light enough to not weigh me down after.

What I love most? They’re high in protein and actually help keep cravings at bay—especially on days when I’m trying to eat a bit cleaner without sacrificing flavor.

I usually serve them with a crisp Cucumber Salad for a cooling crunch and a fresh Raspberry Mojito to balance the heat with minty sweetness. They’re wildly satisfying, easy to make, and just as perfect for a weeknight as they are for game day spreads.

Prep Time Cook Time Marination Time Calories Servings
15 Minutes 10 Minutes 25 Minutes 242kcal 6

  • killet or frying pan (preferably non-stick or cast iron)
  • Mixing Bowl
  • Spoon or Spatula
  • Tongs
  • Slotted spoon (for frying)
  • Plate or tray lined with paper towels
  • Knife and chopping board

  • 8 ounces cooked chopped chicken
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon diced red bell pepper
  • 1 tablespoon diced jalapeño pepper
  • 1 minced garlic clove
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon ground cayenne pepper
  • ¾ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • ¼ cup cream cheese 2 ounces
  • ¾ cup cheddar-jack shredded cheese blend
  • 10 flour tortillas 7-inch
  • 3-4 cups vegetable oil or peanut oil
Serving
  • 3 roughly chopped romaine lettuce
  • 1 medium roma tomato chopped
  • Dipping sauce of choice

  1. Heat 1 tablespoon oil in a skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add diced red bell pepper, jalapeño, and minced garlic; sauté for 2–3 minutes.
  3. Stir in cooked chopped chicken, paprika, cayenne, salt, and black pepper. Mix well.
  4. Add cream cheese and shredded cheese blend; stir until melted and creamy. Remove from heat.
  5. Spoon the mixture into tortillas and roll them tightly into wraps.
  6. Heat 3–4 cups oil in a deep skillet or pan for frying.
  7. Fry wraps until golden brown and crispy, turning once.
  8. Drain on paper towels and serve hot with chopped lettuce, tomato, and dipping sauce.
