Strawberry Cream Cheese Muffins

Soft and buttery with a gentle tang from the cream cheese center, these muffins strike a delicious balance between dessert and breakfast.

What really makes this recipe shine, though, is the built-in portion control you get all the flavors of a cheesecake-strawberry mashup in a single grab-and-go muffin. No slicing, no guilt.

And it turns out, I’m not the only one loving this combo fruit-filled muffins with creamy centers have surged in popularity, with search interest for “strawberry cream cheese muffins” growing over 40% in the past year.

Prep Time Cook Time Total Time Calories Servings
15 Minutes 20 Minutes 35 Minutes 285kcal 12

  • 2 Mixing Bowls
  • Hand mixer or whisk
  • Measuring cups and spoons
  • Muffin pan
  • Paper muffin liners
  • Rubber spatula or spoon
  • Fork (for crumb topping)
  • Oven

Crumbles:
  • 60 grams 6 tablespoons all-purpose flour, dip and sweep
  • 60 grams ¼ cup and 1 tablespoon granulated sugar
  • 42 grams 3 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted*
Cream Cheese Filling:
  • 170 grams 6 oz cream cheese
  • 30 grams 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • ½ teaspoon pure vanilla extract
Muffin Batter:
  • 235 grams 1 and ⅔ cups all-purpose flour, dip and sweep
  • 2 and ½ teaspoons baking powder
  • 2 large eggs room temperature
  • 105 grams ½ cup granulated sugar
  • 115 grams ½ cup unsalted butter, melted*
  • 120 grams ½ cup milk, room temperature
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • 180 grams 1 cup strawberries, chopped into small pieces

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C) and line a muffin tin with paper liners.
  2. Mix crumble topping ingredients in a bowl until crumbly; set aside.
  3. In another bowl, beat cream cheese, sugar, and vanilla until smooth.
  4. In a separate large bowl, whisk flour, baking powder, and sugar.
  5. Add eggs, melted butter, milk, and vanilla to dry ingredients; stir until just combined.
  6. Fold in chopped strawberries gently.
  7. Spoon half the batter into each muffin liner, add a spoonful of cream cheese filling, then top with remaining batter.
  8. Sprinkle with crumble topping and bake for 22–25 minutes or until golden and set.
